Trading & Funding

Trading and funding in the carbon market refer to the buying and selling of carbon credits or allowances and the financial mechanisms used to support projects that reduce greenhouse gas emissions. The carbon market is a system designed to help countries and organizations reduce their carbon emissions by creating a financial incentive for emission reductions.
